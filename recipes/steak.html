Steak

DESCRIPTION

Steak is a cut of meat, most commonly beef, that is typically sliced perpendicular to the muscle fibers.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless ribeye steaks
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2-4 sprigs fresh thyme or rosemary(optional)
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled and lightly crushed(optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the steaks:Remove steaks from refrigerator 20-30 minutes before cooking to bring them close to room temperature. Pat them dry with paper towels.
  2. Season:Preheat your oven to 400 Â°F. Rub both sides of each steak with the olive oil, then season generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Sear:Heat a cast iron or other oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat until it is very hot. Carefully place the steaks in the hot pan. Sear for 1-2 minutes on the first sides until a brown crust forms.
  4. Flip and Finish:Flip the steak and sear the second side for another 1-2 minutes . Remove the skillet from the stovetop. Top each steak with 1 tablespoon of butter and add thyme/rosemary and garlic cloves to the pan.
  5. Bake:Immediately transfer the skillet to the preheated oven. Bake for 10-15 minutes, depending on the thickness of your steak and desired doneness.
  6. Rest and Serve:Use a meat thermometer to check the internal temperature. Remove the steaks from the pan and let them rest on a plate for 5-10 minutes before slicing to lock in the juices.
